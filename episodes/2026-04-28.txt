Two huge sub-storylines. The first:

Matvei Michkov was a healthy scratch for the Flyers, replaced by rookie Alex Bump, who scored his first playoff goal.

Tocchet's decision to bench Michkov is one of the boldest in-series moves of his coaching career here. The second sub-storyline: the rookie phenom Porter Martone.

Martone is a 19-year-old rookie forward and the No. 6 pick in the 2025 NHL Draft, and he became the 12th teenager in NHL history, and the first for Philadelphia, to score in each of his first two postseason games.

He has been everything the Flyers hoped for in terms of poise. The Game 6 environment Wednesday at Xfinity Mobile Arena is going to be his first elimination home game, and that is a real test for a teenager.
